Welcome, Candidate JEE (Main) 2026  |  Paper 1 (B.E. / B.Tech)

📋 General Instructions

Exam Overview

  • The exam consists of 3 subjects — Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Each subject carries 25 questions and 100 marks.
  • Total number of questions: 75  |  Total marks: 300.
  • Total duration of the exam is 3 Hours (180 minutes).

Question Paper Pattern

  • The question paper consists of 3 sections — Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • Each section has 25 questions: 20 MCQ (Single Correct) + 5 Numerical Value questions.
  • For MCQ questions, each question has 4 options with only one correct answer.
  • For Numerical questions, the answer is a non-negative integer to be entered using the virtual keyboard.
  • Total duration of the exam is 3 hours (180 minutes).

Marking Scheme

  • For MCQ (Single Correct) questions: +4 marks for a correct answer and −1 mark for a wrong answer.
  • For Numerical Value questions: +4 marks for a correct answer and no negative marking for a wrong answer.
  • No marks are deducted for unattempted questions in either type.

General Guidelines

  • Do not close or refresh the browser window during the exam.
  • The clock on the top right shows the remaining time. The exam will auto-submit when time is up.
  • You can navigate between questions freely using the Question Palette on the right.
  • Use "Mark for Review" to revisit a question later. Marked questions will still be evaluated.
  • To change an answer, simply click a different option. To deselect, click "Clear Response".
  • Questions are colour-coded in the palette: Green = Answered, Red = Not Answered, Purple = Marked for Review.
  • You can switch between sections at any time during the exam.

Important Rules

  • Use of calculators, mobile phones, smartwatches, or any electronic devices is strictly prohibited.
  • Rough work should be done on the rough sheet provided at the exam centre only.
  • Any malpractice or unfair means will lead to immediate cancellation of candidature.
  • Do not leave the exam hall without the invigilator's permission.
  • Your responses are automatically saved. There is no separate save button.
